The BoSS project seeks to simplify the creation of legally-compliant, blockchain-secured applications that maintain confidentiality and privacy. The consortium developed frameworks for technology selection, supporting middleware, and regulatory compliance.

Within BoSS, Noesis and KU Leuven demonstrated the potential of private blockchains to ensure auditability of data exchange for cross-company multidisciplinary optimization processes in aircraft design.
